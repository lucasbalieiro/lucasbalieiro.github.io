These are personal notes of things I'm learning in `Deutsch`. Beware that this is not reliable, I might be wrong. DYOR.

## Nouns
* every noun seems to start with capital letters, even mid sentence.
* and every single one has a gender
    * masculine: der Man
    * feminine:  die Frau
    * neuter: das Mädchen
    * plural: die Männer, die Frauen, die Mädchen

* another pattern seems that when you add `-in` to a noun, it turns into feminine.
    * der Kollege - die Kolleg*in*
    * der Freund - die Freund*in*
