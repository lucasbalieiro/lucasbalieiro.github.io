Like most people, I tend to define myself by what I do for a living: I’m a software developer.

That’s the simplest way to describe what I do, but it’s not the whole picture. I’m naturally drawn to many different interests at once, and I don’t always manage them as well as I’d like. This blog is my way of keeping track of them. A place to store thoughts, not just about my work and technical topics, but also things I’m learning, experiences I’m having, and books I’m reading.
